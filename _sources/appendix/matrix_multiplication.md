# Matrix Multiplication

```{div} theorem
Let

$$
A = \begin{bmatrix} & & \\ \boldsymbol{a}_1 & \cdots & \boldsymbol{a}_n \\ & & \end{bmatrix}
\hspace{20mm}
B = \begin{bmatrix} & \boldsymbol{b}_1^T & \\ & \vdots & \\ & \boldsymbol{a}_n^T & \end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
AB = \sum_{k=1}^n \boldsymbol{a}_k \boldsymbol{b}_k^T
$$
```
